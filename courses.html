Civil Engineering

📅Duration: 3 Years
💺Seats: 60
🎓Board: MSBTE
📚Eligibility: SSC Pass

The Diploma in Civil Engineering trains students in structural design, construction technology, and site management. With a focus on AutoCAD, surveying, and project management, graduates are well-prepared for opportunities in government infrastructure, real estate, and construction.

Key Subjects

Structural Analysis & Design
Surveying & Geomatics
Construction Materials & Tech
AutoCAD for Civil
Soil Mechanics & Foundation
Water Supply & Sanitation
Quantity Estimation & Costing
Final Year Project

Career Paths

Site Engineer AutoCAD Draftsman Surveyor Govt. Contractor Higher Studies (B.E.)
After Your Diploma

Lateral Entry to B.E. / B.Tech

After completing your diploma from MIT Polytechnic, you are eligible for Direct Second Year (DSY) admission to B.E. / B.Tech programs at any engineering college in Maharashtra — giving you a head-start in your degree without repeating the first year.
