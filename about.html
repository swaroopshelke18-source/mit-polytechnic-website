Our Institution

Who We Are

MIT Polytechnic is a government-approved diploma engineering institution affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE), located in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Maharashtra.

We offer four three-year diploma programs in AI & ML, Computer, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering. Our focus is on practical, hands-on education that prepares students for immediate employment.

Graduates from MIT Polytechnic are also eligible for lateral entry (Direct Second Year) admission to B.E./B.Tech programs across Maharashtra.

Infrastructure

Campus Facilities

Modern, well-maintained facilities to support holistic learning and student life.

💻

Computer & AI Lab

50+ workstations with high-speed internet, Python, MATLAB, AutoCAD, and licensed AI/ML software for all students.

📚

Central Library

5,000+ engineering books, national journals, NPTEL digital access, and a quiet study zone for focused reading.

⚙️

Mechanical Workshop

Industry-standard lathes, milling machines, welding stations, and CNC trainers for practical mechanical work.

⚡

Electrical & Electronics Lab

Oscilloscopes, function generators, circuit trainers, and PLC panels for hands-on electrical experiments.

📐

Civil Survey Lab

Total stations, auto levels, theodolites, and GPS devices for civil surveying and field measurement practicals.

🎤

Seminar Hall

A 200-seat hall with AV equipment used for guest lectures, technical events, workshops, and examinations.
