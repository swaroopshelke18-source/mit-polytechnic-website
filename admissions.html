Admissions 2024–25

Seats are limited. Follow the steps below and secure your future in engineering.

Step-by-Step

Admission Process

1

Check Eligibility

Ensure you've passed SSC (10th) from a recognised board with Mathematics and Science subjects.

2

Register on DTE Portal

Visit dtemaharashtra.gov.in and register for the Polytechnic Online Admission (POLYCET) process.

3

Fill Preference Form

Select MIT Polytechnic and your preferred diploma branch among your college and course preferences.

4

Document Verification

Report to the facilitation centre after merit list publication with original and photocopied documents.

5

Confirm & Pay Fees 🎉

Accept the allotment, pay the tuition fee, and receive your admission confirmation letter. Welcome to MIT Polytechnic!

Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

Criterion Requirement
Qualification SSC (10th) or equivalent
Min. Marks 35% aggregate (no limit for SC/ST)
Compulsory Subjects Maths & Science (for ME & CE)
Age Limit Min. 14 years; no upper limit
Domicile Maharashtra preferred; merit basis for others
Lateral Entry ITI (NCVT) holders eligible for 2nd year
📢 Note: Admissions are centralised through DTE Maharashtra's online process. Direct walk-in admissions are not available. Reserved category benefits apply as per Govt. of Maharashtra norms.

Documents Required

  • ✔ SSC Marksheet & Passing Certificate
  • ✔ School Leaving Certificate (TC)
  • ✔ Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • ✔ Domicile / Nationality Certificate
  • ✔ Aadhar Card (identity proof)
  • ✔ Passport-size Photographs (4 copies)
